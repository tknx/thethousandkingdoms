The Thousand Kingdoms

Long ago, magic began to fade. In an act of desperation it was sealed away. But now those seals are failing and magic returns to the world. Ella, a physics grad student in NYC, is thrown into the deep end and must find a way to reconcile the modern and the magical.

01.012 Halloween

Posted on Thursday, December 16, 2021

A bit more training montage, but I wanted to bring back in some magic and even a sense of wonder. I feel like I missed the mark a bit. But that is okay.

I am not a hard magic guy for the most part despite some of my rpg type posts - my eyes glaze over at most hard magic systems, so I prefer it to be a bit ineffable.

That will get harder as we get to learning more about magic, I want to have the system underneath to keep it somewhat real, but to the reader it needs to feel like, well, magic.
