The Thousand Kingdoms

Long ago, magic began to fade. In an act of desperation it was sealed away. But now those seals are failing and magic returns to the world. Ella, a physics grad student in NYC, is thrown into the deep end and must find a way to reconcile the modern and the magical.

Realm of Dark Sky

Hell of Upside Down Sinners
Unknown location A Hidden Kingdom that is entirely water, some of the Akarthos were trapped here and drowned. The creatures that live here are jelly-fish like but almost invisible and highly, highly toxic. Those creatures that are stung by them actually become longer-lived but live in excruciating agony.
The Hidden Kingdom of Stone
Unknown location A solid stone world, the stone is actually becoming sentient and will be fully aware in roughly another twenty thousand years. The third world where Akarthos ended up dying.
The Killing Gardens
Through a basement in Tokyo, Japan The home of the Keres who serve their own goddess, Nyx. The gardens are filled with beautiful medicinal plants and the Keres live in nature, taking considerable care of their ecosystem from both a sustainability and aesthetic perspective.
