The Thousand Kingdoms

Long ago, magic began to fade. In an act of desperation it was sealed away. But now those seals are failing and magic returns to the world. Ella, a physics grad student in NYC, is thrown into the deep end and must find a way to reconcile the modern and the magical.

Dwarves

Dwarf are 120 to 140cm (4’ to 4'6) and appear to be roughly chiseled out of stone and rock. Their clans are organized by rough colorations although intermarriage is common. They are agendered and reproduce through some form of recombinative technique that reconstitutes the parents into multiple offspring. They do mate for life and will not take another mate.
