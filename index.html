The Thousand Kingdoms
Something has broken and magic returns to the world, transformed by modern life and broken into small pieces. Ella, a physics graduate student, stumbles into fate through a break in our reality and must somehow bridge the two sides in the coming conflict.

A few notes

This book is published mostly when I have time to write, which is irregular since I have a day job. Ignore the footnotes if you would like, nothing critical will go in there, just random stuff.
