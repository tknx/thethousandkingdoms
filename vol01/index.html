The orcs began to try and take their dead with them, and those that were interrogated subscribed to some sort of complicated belief around sanctity if their dead, the Aqrabuamelu’s acts a desecration of the highest order. The forest burned and burned. Ella kept feeding it with magic and the aberrent weather patterns she had set up were now the power of an avalanche.

  • Ella was woken abruptly by Zaidu, she had fallen asleep in her gear, her mask comfortable against her face. It was night and the moonlight lit the fire-strewn field from the quick encampment they had built. They were near the castle now. She could almost hear the pennants. And across that field stood the Emperor and orcs flanking him. These orcs were better armed, looked harder. Elites. Ella stood up, despite the hiss from Zaidu, she could feel her crown settle on her head and that ethereal connection to the crown of the Emperor.
